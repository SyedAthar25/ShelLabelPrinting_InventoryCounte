Troubleshooting Tips:

  • Make sure your printer is turned ON and in pairing mode
  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your device
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting if devices don't appear
  • Check that you're using Chrome or Edge browser
  • Look for devices with names starting with "GP-", "Gprinter", or "GP"
